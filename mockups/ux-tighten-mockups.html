Summary of All Changes
These 10 mockups tighten the app by:
1. Increasing size of primary actions (START button, strip items)
2. Promoting key info to hero positions (total duration, pill badge, stats)
3. Improving contrast on labels (#333 → #555) so they're actually readable
4. Adding visual affordances (chevrons, filled selected states)
5. Grouping related items (settings sections) for scannability
6. Celebrating completion with bigger elements and stat cards
